Major Contributors to Air Pollution

Vehicle Emissions

Exhaust from cars and trucks release nitrogen dioxide, carbon monoxide, and particulate matter into the atmosphere.

Industrial Activities

Factories emit pollutants like sulfur dioxide and volatile organic compounds (VOCs) into the air.

Burning Fossil Fuels

Coal, oil, and gas combustion for energy and heat production release harmful gases and particles.

Agricultural Practices

Pesticides and fertilizers emit ammonia, while burning crop residues adds to air contamination.

Household Activities

Use of solid fuels for cooking and heating can produce significant indoor and outdoor air pollution.

Waste Burning

Burning plastic and other waste emits toxic chemicals including dioxins and furans into the atmosphere.
