Effective Ways to Reduce Air Pollution

  • Use public transportation, carpool, or cycle to reduce vehicle emissions.
  • Switch to renewable energy sources like solar or wind at home and work.
  • Conserve energy by turning off lights and appliances when not in use.
  • Avoid burning trash, plastic, and leaves to reduce release of toxic smoke.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances and switch to LED lighting.
  • Support and follow local laws and regulations on air quality standards.
  • Plant trees and support green spaces which naturally filter air pollutants.
  • Encourage industries to adopt cleaner technologies and pollution controls.
