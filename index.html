Understanding and Fighting Air Pollution

What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution refers to the presence of harmful or excessive quantities of substances in the Earth's atmosphere. These substances—including gases, particles, and biological molecules—can endanger human health, damage ecosystems, and contribute to climate change. The major contributors are industrial emissions, vehicle exhaust, fossil fuel burning, and natural events like wildfires and dust storms.
